K.K.Wagh Polyetechnic

The insitute was established in the year 1983 at Bhausaheab Nagar, Tal.Niphad,Dist. Nashik, Later shifted to Hirabai Haridas Vidyanagari, Nashik in 1992. It Is the only institute which has received "Best Polyetechnic in Maharshtra"Award for best academic performance & non-academic activities for three years. The insitute has been ranked in silver "Platinum Category" in AICTE and CII Survey in the year 2016 & 2017.

It is fully developed and has excellent infrastructure with sprawing lush green campus named Hirabai Haridas Vidyanagaris. It is private college. In this college are many facillities available.
